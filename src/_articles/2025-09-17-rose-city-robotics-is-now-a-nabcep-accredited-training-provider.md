**Real Credentials. Real Solar. Real Impact.**

We’re excited to share a major milestone in our mission to build tools—and careers—that matter. The North American Board of Certified Energy Professionals (NABCEP) has approved Waivolt Trade School by Rose City Robotics as an Accredited Training Provider.

**As an officially accredited NABCEP Training Provider** , Rose City Robotics is authorized to offer:

- The **NABCEP PV Associate Credential** , a widely respected certification for electricians and technicians entering or advancing in the solar field

- **NABCEP Continuing Education Units (CEUs)** for professionals maintaining full certification

This recognition affirms what we’ve worked hard to prove: Rose City Robotics is a **real trade school institution** , committed to technical rigor, hands-on learning, and workforce relevance—especially in the clean energy and engineering space.

**🏛️ What Accreditation Really Means**

Becoming a NABCEP-accredited provider isn’t a simple form and a fee—it’s a rigorous, multi-step review process led by one of the most respected credentialing bodies in the energy sector.   
  
NABCEP ( **North American Board of Certified Energy Practitioners** ) sets the **national gold standard for training for electricians, solar installers and battery technicians** , ensuring that only institutions with strong educational practices, qualified instructors, ethical practices and documented outcomes can offer its credentials.

Our approval required proof of:

- **Instructor qualifications and technical depth**

- **Hands-on curriculum with field-relevant skills**

- **Transparent student policies, assessments, and outcomes**

- **Alignment with evolving workforce and licensing needs**

In short: **they checked everything. And we passed.**

This is a statement of quality, vetted by the same organization trusted by contractors, inspectors, and utilities nationwide.

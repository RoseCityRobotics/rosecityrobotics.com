At Rose City Robotics, we are committed to fostering a vibrant, grassroots community for robotics professionals in the Pacific Northwest. We are excited to announce that we will be hosting the [Rose City edition of the Robotics Collaborative meetup](https://lu.ma/wayo5yb9) on Wednesday, March 26, 2025, from 5:30 PM to 7:30 PM PDT at the Metro Region Innovation Hub in Portland, Oregon.

### Event Highlights

- **Lightning Talks:** Hear from five distinguished local experts, including:
  - David Nichols, Co-founder & CEO of Loupe
  - Robert Topple, Founder & CEO of Electron Robotics
  - Joseph Cole, Founder & CTO of Rose City Robotics
  - Spencer Lindsay, Founder of E-tribe
  - Erin Galiger, Director of North American Markets at ROCSYS

- **Diverse Audience:** Connect with a wide range of attendees, from CEOs and academic researchers to startups, investors, and engineers.
- **Panel Discussion and Q&A:** Engage in an interactive session with our speakers, addressing audience questions and exploring current trends in robotics.
- **Networking Opportunities:** Build relationships with fellow robotics enthusiasts, entrepreneurs, and experts to explore potential collaborations and innovations.

#### Event Schedule

- 5:30 PM – 6:00 PM: Check-in
- 6:00 PM – 6:15 PM: Introductions and agenda overview
- 6:15 PM – 6:35 PM: Lightning talks
- 6:35 PM – 6:50 PM: Speaker panel and audience Q&A
- 6:50 PM – 7:00 PM: Audience announcements, resources, and job opportunities
- 7:00 PM – 7:30 PM: Informal networking

### About the Robotics Collaborative

This initiative brings together robotics entrepreneurs, scientists, machine learning experts, and enthusiasts with the mission to establish the Pacific Northwest as a leading hub for robotics innovation. Our collaborative meetings aim to facilitate the exchange of ideas, spark new partnerships, and explore opportunities to launch groundbreaking robotics technologies.

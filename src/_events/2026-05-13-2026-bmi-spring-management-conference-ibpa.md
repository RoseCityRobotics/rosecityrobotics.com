## Conference Overview

The BMI Spring Management Conference will be held May 13–14 at the Hyatt Regency Portland in conjunction with IBPA. Besides enjoying the BMI Spring Conference, attendees will have the chance to stay in Portland to learn and network with IBPA members.

### Featuring Rose City Robotics

On Thursday, May 14 from 1:00–2:00 PM, our own **Joe Cole** will present _"AI, Robotics and Automation in Manufacturing"_.

### Stay for IBPA

One of the benefits of co-locating with IBPA is the option to stay for the IBPA Publishing University event. When you register for the BMI Spring Management Conference, you'll see options to add on 1-day or 2-day passes to IBPA. It features 3 keynote speakers and 5 breakout sessions dedicated to Established Publishers, Newer Publishers, and Industry Hot Topics.

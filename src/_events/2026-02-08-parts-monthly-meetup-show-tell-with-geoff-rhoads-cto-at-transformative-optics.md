**What:** Portland Area RoboTics Society (PARTS) Meetup  
**When:** Sunday, February 8 · 10:30 AM  
 **Where:** Control H / PDX Hackerspace — 7608 N Interstate Ave, Portland, OR • [pdxhackerspace.org](https://pdxhackerspace.org/)  
  
 **Note:** PARTS is meeting a week later than usual this month. Joe Cole coordinated this session with Geoff Rhodes.

**What’s happening**  
 Geoff Rhoads, Founder and CTO at [Transformative Optics Corporation](https://www.transformativeoptics.com/) will run a **show-and-tell** style session walking through array camera images, sample outputs, and several of the company’s custom lens designs. Expect a conversational flow with pauses for questions as the visuals come up, plus a 5–15 minute open Q&A at the end.   
  
This format is ideal if you work with (or are curious about) **robotics, computer vision, imaging systems, or novel optical hardware** and want to see real hardware and data, not just diagrams.

**Why you should come**

- Get a behind-the-scenes look at unconventional optics and what they mean for practical CV/robotics work.

- Ask implementation-level questions while examples are on screen.

- Compare tradeoffs you’ve seen with what Geoff’s team is building.

**Who it’s for**  
 Engineers, builders, and curious folks across experience levels.

**Logistics**

- Doors at 10:20; talk starts 10:30.

- Control H / PDX Hackerspace: [https://pdxhackerspace.org/](https://pdxhackerspace.org/)
